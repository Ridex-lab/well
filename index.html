Why I Created This Guide

For years, I struggled with recurring feminine discomfort that kept coming back no matter what I tried. Sometimes the symptoms would disappear for a while, only to return again weeks later. It became frustrating, exhausting, and honestly embarrassing to keep dealing with.

I started paying closer attention to my body, daily habits, stress levels, diet, hygiene products, and even some of the traditional wellness practices older African women around me quietly recommended.

At first, I didn’t take some of those methods seriously. But after combining natural wellness habits, feminine care routines, better nutrition, and more intentional self-care, I slowly began noticing real changes.

I documented everything that genuinely helped me — the routines, foods, and natural practices that made the biggest difference. That personal journey eventually became this guide.
